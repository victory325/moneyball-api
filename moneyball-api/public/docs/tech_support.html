iOS

iOS Device Compatibility

To successfully play the $BALL App, we recommend these iOS devices:

  • iPhone 5 or higher
  • iPad 3/iPad Mini 2 or higher
  • iPod Touch 6 or higher

Requires iOS 9.0 or later. iOS devices with less than 1 GB RAM may encounter more issues while playing the $BALL Mobile App. We recommend using devices with more than 1 GB RAM.

ANDROID

Android Device Minimum Requirements

To successfully play the $BALL App on an Android device ,we recommend that the device meet or exceed these minimum requirements:

  • Android 5.0 or higher
  • 2 GB of RAM or higher
  • 2 GHz Dual Core Processor or higher

GENERAL

Troubleshooting Tips

Players may experience technical issues from time to time while playing the $BALL App due to numerous factors. Here are some general troubleshooting tips that may lessen the occurrence of technical problems:

  • Disable any unnecessary programs or streaming services on your device while playing.
  • Restart your device/connection to WiFi or network on a regular basis.
  • Try switching from WiFi to 3G/4G LTE and vice versa.
  • Make sure that your connection to the internet is secure and is working correctly.
  • Update the operating system of your device (if an update is available).
  • Disable Airplane Mode (if enabled).

If you encounter technical difficulties while playing a cash tournament - DO NOT immediately or continuously re-enter the tournament - reboot your device, start a brand new game session, or play a Warm-Up (free) Individual Play round to test your device.

$BALL cannot be held responsible for game errors/issues caused by factors external to our mobile servers.
